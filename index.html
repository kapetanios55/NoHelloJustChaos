Pro tips for maximum disruption

  • Never provide context. They should be monitoring your every thought. It’s called teamwork.
  • Master the message fragment. Why say it once when you can say it seven times in rapid succession with decreasing coherence?
  • Tag liberally. @here is just a fun way to say "I need attention RIGHT NOW from people who don’t care."
  • Respect no boundaries. 4:59 PM Friday? Perfect. Their PTO? Even better. Their wedding day? Chef’s kiss.
Effective communication is overrated. What really matters is asserting dominance through confusion.
